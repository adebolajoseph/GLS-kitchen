Who We Are

A Place Where Flavour Meets Community

GLS Kitchen & Bakery was born out of a simple but powerful idea — that great food brings people together. Nestled along Epe–Ijebu Ode Road, beside the familiar landmark of Foltar Filling Station, we opened our doors to serve the local community and travellers alike with honest, wholesome Nigerian cuisine.

From the very beginning, our kitchen has been guided by one principle: cook every meal the way you would for family. That means no shortcuts, no compromises — just fresh ingredients, traditional recipes and the kind of care that makes food taste like home.

Over the years, we have earned the trust and loyalty of hundreds of customers, reflected in our 4.3★ Google rating and the smiles we see every single day. Whether you stop in for a quick bite or settle in for a full meal, GLS Kitchen & Bakery is always glad to have you.
