Who We Are

A Taste of Home,
Every Single Day

GLS Kitchen & Bakery is a beloved dining spot nestled along Epe–Ijebu Ode Road, beside Foltar Filling Station. We serve freshly prepared Nigerian meals and artisan baked goods that warm the soul.

From our smoky jollof rice to our fresh-baked meat pies, every dish is made with the finest local ingredients and a whole lot of love.
